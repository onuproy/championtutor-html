Learning Science from the Beginning

Is Science important? Well, it’s one of the four compulsory subjects that students have to learn starting from the Primary level. As students progress to the Secondary Level, they have to take not just one, but two or more pure or combined science subjects sitting for their N or O level examinations.

Learning in the classroom has never been a one-size-fits-all solution. In an environment where 40 students vie for 1 teacher’s attention, it is no wonder that some kids fall behind. The problem with Science is that when a student fails to grasp a particular science topic or formula, it is difficult to keep up as the teacher moves on to the next topic.

Failing in science will not just affect your child in their PSLE, O-levels, and A-levels. It can severely limit their choices when they head to polytechnic or university, where there is a significant number of courses involved in Science and require good science grades such as Mechanical Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and even Physics, Biological Science, and Medicine.
